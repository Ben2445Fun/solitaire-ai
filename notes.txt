All cards are listed as a number between 1 and 52
The seven visible cards are: x0, x7, x15, x18, x22, x25, and x27
All cards 29-52 are visible in the deck
The suits are in the following order: Spades, Clubs, Hearts, Diamonds
The number divided by 13 rounded down will give the suit of the card (0, 1, 2, or 3)
The number modulo 13 will give the value of the card (0-12)
WARNING: An ace of the previous suit will be marked as the next suit. Make sure to either decrease all values by 1 or check for an ace
